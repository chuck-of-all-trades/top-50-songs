Ghost of Perdition

Opeth

The sonic terrain covered on this track is mind-boggling. Beauty meets brutality. Death growls giving way to beautiful acoustic arpeggios. A whole bunch of ideas and riffs that seem random individually before coming together in a dramatic climax. Masterful songwriting, brilliant execution. My favourite song of all time, and I'm reminded why on each listen.

Key moments:

0:07 We have lift-off
2:30 This transition is like a rocky patch giving way to a calming meadow. Wait for that arpeggio!
7:57 Just a beautiful, well-timed, well-spaced solo. Nothing legendary, but just perfect for this segment and song.
8:19 The growls are terrific, but the guitars in the background make this section for me. This is the kind of throwaway stuff that just makes me fall in love with a song even more. The outro from here is sheer bliss - every single idea in the song so far just comes together.
